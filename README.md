# Advanced Temporal Modeling in Healthcare

Research-oriented course on time series analysis for healthcare systems, with a strong focus on mathematical foundations, irregular medical data, and clinical decision modeling.

---
🚧 This repository is under active development — first materials coming soon.
---

## Overview

This repository contains the material for an advanced course dedicated to temporal modeling in healthcare.

Unlike standard time series courses, this project focuses on:

* mathematically grounded modeling (stochastic processes, functional analysis)
* irregular and event-based medical data
* multimodal temporal signals (physiological, clinical, epidemiological)
* causal reasoning and intervention modeling
* clinical interpretability and decision-making

The objective is to bridge the gap between:

* theoretical time series analysis
* and real-world healthcare systems

## Planned Structure

The course is organized into six main parts:

1. Mathematical Foundations of Temporal Modeling
2. Statistical Structure of Healthcare Time Series
3. Irregular and Event-Based Medical Data
4. Learning and Representation in Temporal Health Data
5. Causality, Interventions, and Clinical Decisions
6. Frontier Research Topics

## Repository Structure

* `docs/` — lecture notes, slides, and figures
* `notebooks/` — practical sessions and experiments
* `data/` — datasets (raw and processed)
* `models/` — modeling approaches and implementations
* `evaluation/` — metrics and validation frameworks
* `clinical_context/` — domain-specific medical contexts
* `publications/` — research directions and drafts

---

## Objectives

This project aims to:

* provide a rigorous and modern introduction to temporal modeling in healthcare
* highlight the limitations of classical time series methods in medical contexts
* introduce advanced tools for irregular, multimodal, and intervention-driven data
* support research and publication in this area

---

## Target Audience

* Master's and PhD students
* Researchers in machine learning, statistics, or healthcare
* Data scientists working with medical time series
